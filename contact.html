Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to help you plan your perfect stay at HOTEL COZZY INN

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in begins at 2:00 PM and check-out is by 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out are available on request, subject to room availability.

Is airport / railway station transfer available?

Yes, we offer complimentary transfers for Premium Suite guests. Other room categories can arrange transfers at an additional charge — please mention at booking.

Do you have on-site dining?

Our in-house restaurant serves Rajasthani specialties and continental cuisine. A rooftop café with city views is open for breakfast and evening snacks. Room service is available 24/7.

What is your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 48 hours before check-in. Cancellations within 48 hours are charged one night's stay. Special promotional rates may have different terms.

Do you host weddings and events?

Absolutely! We have dedicated banquet and lawn spaces for weddings, corporate events, and private celebrations. Contact us for customised packages and pricing.

Is the spa and wellness centre open to all guests?

Yes, our award-winning spa is open to all hotel guests. Treatments include traditional Ayurvedic therapies, aromatherapy, and modern wellness sessions. Advance booking is recommended.
