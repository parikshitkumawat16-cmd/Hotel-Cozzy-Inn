About Us

Your Cozy Stay in Chomu

HOTEL COZZY INN is built with a vision to provide guests with a comfortable, clean, and affordable stay experience. Conveniently located near Bhojlawa Puliya on Chomu Bypass, our hotel is ideal for travelers, families, and business guests.

We believe in simple hospitality with attention to detail making your stay peaceful and memorable.

HOTEL COZZY INN focuses on delivering quality service, hygienic food, and modern facilities to ensure every guest feels at home.
